### Step 1: Create a Vercel Account
1. Go to https://vercel.com/signup
2. Sign up with GitHub (recommended) or email
3. It's completely free for personal projects

### Step 2: Deploy Your Site
1. Drag and drop the entire `dwb-website` folder onto Vercel's dashboard
2. Vercel will automatically detect it's a Vite + React project
3. Click "Deploy"
4. Your site will be live in ~2 minutes with a free `.vercel.app` URL

### Step 3: Connect Your Custom Domain
1. In Vercel dashboard, go to your project settings
2. Click "Domains"
3. Add your domain from Wix
4. Follow Vercel's instructions to update DNS settings in Wix
5. Done! Your site will be live on your custom domain
